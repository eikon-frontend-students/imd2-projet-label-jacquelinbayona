SOMEBODY LOVES ME PT. 2 (FEAT. CASH COBAIN)

This remix revisits the original with a denser production, warm bass, and the nocturnal energy typical of the OVO Sound. PND and Drake's ethereal vocals blend with Cash Cobain's raw flow, creating a contrast between tenderness and intimacy. The track explores the need for genuine love beneath the veneer of luxury and success. The lyrics oscillate between melancholy and hedonism, a signature of the Toronto sound. It's an introspective yet sensual track, tailor-made for late nights and contemporary R&B playlists. A reminder that even in the spotlight, OVO always retains a touch of darkness.
